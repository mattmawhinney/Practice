Liz Abinante

programmer (in training). knitter. sassy lady.

Hello! My name is Liz Abinante. I'm in the process of becoming a software engineer at Dev Bootcamp. I'm also lucky enough to have a hobby I love as my side career producing independent knitwear designs. I finish Dev Bootcamp in mid-July 2013, and then I begin the terrifying process of finding the right job as a junior developer.

My super powers are coding, freakishly fast hand raising, speed-reading, and selecting perfectly ripe fruits and vegetables.

In addition to this high level of nerdiness, I also have a double Bachelor's in Psychology and Women's Studies (UC Davis) and a Master's in Women's Studies (San Francisco State University). I wrote my Master's thesis on vampires, it's got a great title: Bite Me, I'm Yours: Vampire Romance, Female Readership, and Negotiating Ideology. Before starting my journey at Dev Bootcamp, my thesis was my proudest accomplishment. If you ask me about it I will talk your ear off about vampires for 2+ hours and offer to send you a copy to read. Just a warning.

I'm currently living in Chicago but am a born-and-raised California girl at heart. Someday I'll move back to California or some other place where things like this don't happen.

Hopefully, someday soon when I'm not at Dev Bootcamp for 15 hours a day, I will have more exciting things here for you. For now, it's just my smiling face and this little bio.
