About Me

My name is Matt Mawhinney, and I am a web developer in training. I already know a little bit of Ruby, HTML, CSS, and jQuery, and from July through September I will be at Dev Bootcamp in Chicago where I will learn Rails, Javascript, how to work in professional development environment, and much more!

For the past two years, I was living and working in sunny Austin, Texas where I simultanesouly caught the tech bug, the music bug, and the Tex-Mex bug. After winning tickets to the Evernote Trunk Conference last August and TEDxAustin in February, I decided I needed to be smack-dab in the middle of the tech industry, solving problems, building products, and putting a dent in the universe.
